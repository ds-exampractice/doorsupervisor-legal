Before You Contact Us

Try these quick solutions first:

App Not Loading?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart the app
  • Update to the latest version
  • Clear app cache (Settings → Apps → Door Supervisor Exam Practice → Storage → Clear Cache)

Can't Log In?

  • Check your email and password are correct
  • Make sure you're using the email you registered with
  • Try the "Forgot Password" option

Purchase Not Working?

  • Make sure you're logged into the correct Google Play account
  • Restart the app and try "Restore Purchases"
  • Check your Google Play purchase history
