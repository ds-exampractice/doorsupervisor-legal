Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen. You'll receive a reset link via email. If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes, once you've downloaded content, you can access practice questions and study materials offline. However, progress syncing and new content downloads require an internet connection.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed through the Google Play Store. Visit your Google Play purchase history and request a refund directly from Google. We cannot process refunds directly.

Is this app officially endorsed by the SIA?

No, our app is an independent study tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Security Industry Authority (SIA). We provide practice materials to help you prepare for the official SIA exam.

How often is content updated?

We regularly review and update our practice questions and study materials to ensure they remain current with SIA exam requirements. Major updates are released quarterly.

Can I use one account on multiple devices?

Yes, you can log into your account on multiple devices. Your progress and settings will sync across all devices when you're connected to the internet.
